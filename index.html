How to play

To play this game you have to create an account and your opponent must create an account as well, once both both accounts are created (you can choose to make more), you must then log in by clicking the "login" link at the bottom of the sign-up form where it says ("have an account? login"). Once logged in, you can head to the game page by clicking the play link.

*To start, if you want a timed mode, click any of the mode options(reload page if you do not want a timed mode) then click start. [blitz is a 10minute mode, Rapid is 5minutes and bullet is 2 minutes]. Time modes are lost by whoever's timer runs out first or who gets checkmated first.
If you end the game before anyone has lost it will count as a draw.
The leaderboard contains the rankings of all players, it can be filtered to, top 3, top 5 and all. By default it shows all player rankings.
